NUS Statistic Society
A student-initiated entity that aims to organize events for NUS students to support their academic endeavors and career pursuit relevant to statistics.
Partner:TerraAI
an AI division of Terra Weather, a weather technology company focusing on developing cutting-edge predictive solutions and AI technology to save lives. Our weather AI, TerraWaves, automatically combines data from multiple sources including satellites and processes them through advanced numerical modelling and AI computation to generate highly accurate weather predictions and risk assessment. TerraAI team has collectively taught over 600+ students practical data science through various initiatives including IoT Datathon, IoT Datathon 2.0, and NUS Statistics Data Science Competition. Other than that, TerraAI has developed powerful AI development tools, meant to dramatically simplify and turbocharge machine learning development for non-programmers.
