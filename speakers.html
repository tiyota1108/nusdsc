Arnold Doray
CEO of TerraWeather

Arnold Doray is an entrepreneur, developer, scientist and educator. He is the Chief Executive Officer of Terra Weather, a leading Singapore-based weather technology company specialized in developing weather forecasting systems for mission-critical offshore operations, serving multinational oil and gas companies worldwide since 2007. Beyond his professional career, Arnold dedicates his time mentoring the next generation of technologists and innovators. Through his major involvement in IoT Datathon and Data Science Competition, Arnold has taught collectively more than 500 students practical AI skills and product development. Arnold is also the creator of Smojo Programming Language, a simple-but-powerful programming language for data science.
