About The Challenge

The Arab Peninsular represents a fast-growing region for global logistics and maritime activity. It facilitates 10% of global trade worth US$1.72 trillion passing through its waters every year. The region is the top energy producer in the world.

The safety and success of these ports and offshore installations depend on timely weather forecasts, which include warnings of squalls, sandstorms and high winds or waves. These natural events can cause loss of life or environmental disasters like oil spills.

In this challenge, you will build a Squall Early Warning System (SEWS) for the Red Sea and Gulf regions. The goal of SEWS is to warn of impending strong winds associated with these weather systems with an ideal lead time of 6 hours. You'll utilise TerraAI's realtime satellite imagery system to drive realtime predictions, training your model on historical data. As a participant you will be invited to a 2-day predictive AI bootcamp by the TerraAI team.
